FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I Need Planning Permission for My Garden Room?

Most of our garden rooms fall within permitted development rights, meaning planning permission is typically not required. However, certain properties or specific requirements may need approval from your local authority. If planning permission is needed, we can manage the entire application process for you as an optional service.

How Well Insulated Are Your Garden Rooms?

Our garden rooms are built using high-quality insulation and premium double-glazed windows to ensure excellent thermal performance all year round. The floors, walls, and roof panels are fully insulated with market-leading materials, helping to keep your space warm in winter and cool in summer.

How Long Does Installation Take?

Our garden rooms are usually installed in around one week, depending on the size and design. If you choose extra features like plastering and decorating, you may need to allow an additional one to two weeks for drying before painting.

Can I Include Dividing Walls or Create Separate Rooms?

Yes, our larger garden rooms can be customised with internal partition walls, allowing you to create multiple rooms or dedicated spaces designed around your requirements.

How Disruptive Is the Installation Process?

We understand that having work carried out at your property can be inconvenient, which is why we aim to keep disruption to a minimum. Our experienced installation team works efficiently and respectfully, ensuring your daily routine is affected as little as possible. We also take great care to protect your property and maintain a clean, tidy work area throughout the installation.

Do your garden rooms come with a guarantee?

All our garden rooms come with a 10-year structural guarantee, giving you complete peace of mind that your investment is protected.
